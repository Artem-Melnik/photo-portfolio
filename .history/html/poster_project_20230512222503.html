Beautiful Paths f/1.5 1/5032 sec ISO-97
I really liked working on this project because creating typography is not something that I have really experimented with previously, so it was nice to be able to do this in my work.


Additionally, the editing that I completed for this image allowed me to learn several new Photoshop skills that I believe will be very helpful when creating other pieces of work, especially if they include working with typography.
For this particular image, I decided to create a style that was a bit more minimalistic and simple, but one that also expressed my editing skills.


To do this, I used a simple font, but edited the image in an interesting way which was close to black and white but not quite. I wanted the yellow lines on the road and the fire hydrant to stand out, as well as the sky above.
