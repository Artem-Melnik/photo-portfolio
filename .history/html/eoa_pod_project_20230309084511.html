Photography Portfolio // EOA/POD Project

In this project, I tried to show the beauty of nature and the outdoors by showing different types of landscapes and locations. In order to share my views with the viewer, I used various editing techniques and interesting color palettes. By illustrating these diverse scenes, I hope to inspire the viewer to enjoy the outdoors and protect our wonderful planet.
